Welcome to the REELER mice gene database

Reelin is an extracellular matrix molecule which binds to two cell surface receptors known as apoER2 and VLDLR. Reelin mediates signals to the interior of cells by binding to these receptors. Intracellular propagation of the signal from the receptors is mediated by an intracellular adaptor protein called Dab1. When either Reelin, Dab1, or both receptors are knocked out in mice, a severe neuronal migration phenotype develops.

The aim of this database is to present data about each individual mutant and also overlapping data from the different Reeler mutants. The user would like to search the database by gene symbol or search criteria.
